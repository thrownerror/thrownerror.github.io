Robert Bailey's Portfolio: Writing

Brief explanation:

My name is Robert Bailey, and I'm a Game Design and Development student attending the Rochester Institute of Technology. This page is for links to my writing samples. My full coding portfolio and bio is located here.

My most relevant fiction writing exists here as part of Game Based Fiction, a class at RIT.

For this class, I write short vignettes, along with character and location bios and histories. These are all set in the Game of Thrones/A Song of Ice and Fire universe.
Most of my writing revolves around my perspective character, Emond Roebeck and House Fenshaw.
The class works as a collaborative effort, with each student contributing to the story as the professor manages the events using the ASOIAF role playing system.

Because of the wiki structure of the class, I'll link there to show my work as part of the larger whole. The above link redirects to my profile on the site, with an updating list of characters, items, and stories I've written, updating with in-class play

While I primarily write in Word and Google Docs, this also allows me to show edits made and work on collaborative pages.
