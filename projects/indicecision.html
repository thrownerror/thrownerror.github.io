inDICEcision

Prev Next

Dice based party game for all ages.
Features multiple ways to play for players to adjust the game to their group.
At it's most basic, players try to have the most points (face up dice pips) on their board. They can choose to roll on their own board, or try to knock points off their opponents' boards by throwing the foam dice.
A drafting mode adds different powers to the dice, and players pick their arsenal before the round starts.
Powers include:

  • A die that subtracts score
  • A die that "burns" other dice, nullifying them
  • A die with a consistent value, but a chance at nothing
  • A die that allows a reroll
  • A die that replaces one on the board

Players can also use included terrain objects, like foam barrels, to create different board arrangements and ways to play.
Rules available here!

Technical Elements:

  • Players: 2-6
  • Recommended Age: 6+
  • Playtime: 10-20 minutes
