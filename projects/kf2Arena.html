Killing Floor 2 - Arena

A decaying, collapsed sports arena for Killing Floor 2, focusing on the survival game mode.
All pathing and geometry handled in the Killing Floor 2 SDK.
All assets and models created inside the Killing Floor 2 editor.
Does not have Maya (or similar 3D model) complete assets. Focused on learning and using the engine.
