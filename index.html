Robert Bailey's Portfolio

Welcome!


My name is Robert Bailey, a game designer currently attending the Game Design and Development Master's Program at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I have a strong passion for telling stories through level design and mechanical implementations. Currently, I'm in my fourth year of my academic program, pursuing dual BS/MS degrees. I am looking for job opportunities in games and general software development as a Level Designer, Gameplay Programmer, Technical Designer, or related fields.
A selection of my projects are hosted on itch.io - An 8-person VR car combat game developed in a semester, VRoom, and a zen game made as part of Global Games 2017, Swingy Dinghy

You can find me on LinkedIn and Twitter!

Current Project:


Continued work on ColorCoded

Continuing development as part of MajorStack Games on the mobile-focused logic puzzle game, ColorCoded. ColorCoded received funding for work over the summmer from Magic Spell Studios, as part of the Co-Up Program. That work continues as we move towards release. Working on continued ports to iOS and Android devices, as well as store implementation. Features over 250 levels.
Developing microtransaction implementation, mobile optimization, and continued refinement of player experience.
Check out MajorStack Games on Twitter for updates!

ColorCoded is now in closed alpha on Google Play and Apple store!
