Breach Initiation


An interactive twine game about attempting to survive a night in the fantasy universe of The Breach.
Player takes on the role of a new recruit attempting to survive the initiation of spending a night in the forest.
Monsters, universe, characters, all constructed by me.
Features 42 different paths (albeit many end in death), with 12 distinct endings.
Playable here, hosted on philome.la!



Technical Elements:

  • 13 Distinct Locations
  • 7 Different monsters
  • Timer-managed logic

Started as a project for a Digital Creative Writing class, expanded far beyond the requirements
