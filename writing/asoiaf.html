Writing - Game Based Fiction


A class roleplaying experience, where the ASOIAF roleplaying system facilitated writing prompts and character interactions for short vignettes.
Collaborative world building inside of the A Song of Ice and Fire Universe, the different classmates all created NPCs and locatiosn for players to visit.
Used a live wiki to facilitate these interactions.
I played a guard for the player-created House Fenshaw as we visited King's Landing.
Portfolio link which connects all of my work on the site: here.



Sample Vignettes

  • The Red Hill The introduction to player character Emond Roebeck and a formative encounter with bandits.
  • Evening Walk The Fenshaw members in King's Landing get attacked by thieves in the street.
  • The Red Hill Emond rides against bandits outside the city.
  • Sins Fully Paid The Fenshaw home gets attacked in the middle of the night.
  • Midnight Meeting An epilogue of sorts for Emond, takes place after the events of the game, using the character's drawn fate as inspiration.
